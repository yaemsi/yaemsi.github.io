About the photographer

A researcher
with an eye.

I'm Yassir — by day an ML Research Engineer, by every other hour a photographer. The same obsession with patterns, light, and meaning that draws me to machine learning pulls me through the viewfinder.

My work spans Morocco and Europe, mixing long documentary projects with spontaneous street photography and commissioned portrait work. Every frame has a reason to exist.
